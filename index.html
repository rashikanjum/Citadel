Mini Games

The mini games are a popular attraction of our chatroom. It consists of the following games:

  • Finding Easter eggs in the chatroom:
    Find anomalies or unusualities within the chatroom. Some are hidden, some go unnoticed. Find them and earn 10 points for each find!

  • Chess:
    Play chess with fellow chatters or any member of the admin dept. Earn 10 points for each round! Send screenshots of your win to any admin and mods for registering your points.

  • Rock, Papers, Scissors:
    Play a round with anyone here at RPS game. Share the link and play with them while chatting for communication. Each win against the player will earn you 0.5 points! Send screenshots to the mods or admins for verification of your win.

  • Quizzes, Riddles, IQ related questions and answers:
    This game will be hosted by admins and mods only. They'll ask you questions, or give you riddles to solve or do an IQ test. Answer them, win, and earn 1 point for each of them.

More games will be added in the future. If you have any suggestions, then mail the founder.

Rewards:

If you reach 500 points, you'll be given Discord Nitro for one month (not basic) or you can choose profile decorations instead (which is a permanent purchase).

The leaderboard will be updated weekly. It'll be reset once someone reaches 500 points. Good luck.
